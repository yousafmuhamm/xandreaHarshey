# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients, partners, job candidates, and community stakeholders evaluating Xandrea Harshey Services Inc. as a diversified Canadian business group. They arrive looking for confidence, legitimacy, operating breadth, leadership credibility, and a clear path to contact the company.

## Product Purpose

This site presents Xandrea Harshey Services Inc. as a premium Calgary-based enterprise spanning construction, facility services, hospitality, international trade, entertainment, and strategic ventures. Success means visitors quickly understand the group's scale and values, trust the organization, explore its companies and leadership, and take the next step through contact, careers, or company-specific inquiry.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, polished, and trust-building. The experience should feel editorial and image-led, with calm confidence, careful craft, and warm human credibility rather than generic corporate noise.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page patterns, dense corporate brochure layouts, synthetic stock-card grids, loud gradient-heavy tech aesthetics, and anything that makes the company feel small, improvised, or template-built. Placeholder imagery should be replaced with branded assets as soon as real photography is available.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with credibility: every page should make the group feel established, accountable, and real.
2. Show breadth without clutter: diversified operations should feel organized, not scattered.
3. Let imagery carry confidence: the site should use strong visual moments instead of over-explaining.
4. Keep motion supportive: reveals, transitions, and media effects should enhance the editorial feel without delaying content.
5. Make the next step obvious: contact, company exploration, and careers paths should stay easy to find.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Preserve semantic landmarks and headings, keyboard access for nav, menus, modals, forms, and chat, descriptive alt text, visible focus states, sufficient contrast, and full `prefers-reduced-motion` support for GSAP, Framer Motion, and any custom animation.
